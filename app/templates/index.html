Welcome to ThermalSim Suite!

This application provides a collection of calculators for common thermal engineering problems. Navigate through the tabs above to access the different simulation tools.

Current tools available:

  • Extended Surface (Fin) Calculator: Analyze the performance of rectangular fins, including temperature distribution, heat transfer rate, and efficiency.
  • Composite Wall Heat Conduction Calculator: Calculate heat flux and thermal resistances for multi-layered composite walls.
  • Parallel/Counter-Flow Heat Exchanger Calculator: Determine the performance of heat exchangers using the NTU-effectiveness method, including outlet temperatures and actual heat transfer.

Select a calculator from the navigation bar to begin.
